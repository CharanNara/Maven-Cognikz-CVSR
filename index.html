ABOUT

A maven (also mavin) is a trusted expert in a particular field, who seeks to pass knowledge on to others. The word maven comes from Hebrew, via Yiddish, and means one who understands, based on an accumulation of knowledge and skilled in any field . Cognikz is a text speech format of cognizant which emphasizes on learning, perception and reasoning. Hence, by coalescing these two words we labeled ourselves as skilled personnel who are in quest of acquiring more and in-depth knowledge and having fun with it The main motto is to improve the personality development of students and to bring out the hidden talents of each of them in any field, also give career guidance. In this academic year we have introduced a new event called “Talent Exhibition” .It is a great way to exhibit the talents of students. Participating in a “Talent Exhibition” gives all students recognition for non‐academic talents. It also provides character‐building lessons during try‐outs, practice and performance.

2018-2019

JAM Session For II year I Semester Students.

Date:1/08/18
This session was taken to students of second year to make them step into the world of communications. The activity ‘Just a minute or JAM’ is an impromptu speech where the speaker is supposed to express the idea (s) on the given topic, within the duration of a minute. It is quite common to see a speaker experiencing nervousness when asked to speak instantly. Effective impromptu speaking is a skill that can be learnt through practice and training. Debate is a process that involves formal discussion on a particular topic. In a debate, opposing arguments are put forward to argue for opposing viewpoints. This session helps the students to think spontaneously and helps them to improve their communication skills
